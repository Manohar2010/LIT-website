About CIE

Where ideas become ventures

The Centre for Innovation & Entrepreneurship is the creative engine of MLR Institute of Technology — a space where students turn curiosity into capability and ideas into impact.

Our Mission

To cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset in every student by providing the tools, mentorship, and ecosystem needed to transform raw ideas into real-world solutions.

Our Vision

To be the most student-driven innovation hub in the country — producing founders, builders, and leaders who shape the future of technology and society.

Our Story

What began as a small group of passionate students has grown into a thriving community of innovators, hosting hackathons, building products, and launching startups that reach beyond campus.

    FAQ

    Questions, answered

    Who can join CIE?

    Any student of MLR Institute of Technology, regardless of year or branch, can apply. We welcome curiosity over experience.

    Do I need prior experience?

    No. We value passion and willingness to learn over existing skills. Our onboarding and workshops will get you up to speed.

    How much time do I need to commit?

    Typically 4–6 hours per week, with more during events and hackathons. We work with your academic schedule.

    Is there a fee to join?

    No. CIE is completely free for all MLR Institute of Technology students.

    Can I switch domains after joining?

    Yes. We encourage exploration. You can switch domains after your first semester with your lead's approval.

    Does CIE help with startups?

    Absolutely. Our Startup Lab provides mentorship, resources, and connections to help you take your idea from zero to launch.
